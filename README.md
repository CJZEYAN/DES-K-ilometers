# DES-K-ilometers

> 一款源自中国民间牌戏“打老K”的现代双人/团队卡牌竞技游戏。
> A modern card duel game derived from the Chinese folk game "Da Lao K".

---

## 📖 游戏简介 / Introduction

**Des K**，全称 **Des kilometers**，是一款基于中国淮南地区传统民间扑克玩法“打老K”进行现代竞技化重构的卡牌对战游戏。它保留了民间牌桌上一招致命的博弈感，同时引入了简明的规则机制，实现了极高的竞技性与策略深度。

而“打老K”的底层血脉，实际上源自**俄罗斯的国牌游戏——杜拉克（Durak）**。《Des K》通过规则改良，将这种跨越国界、扎根中国民间的卡牌对抗逻辑，提炼成了现代的竞技框架。

**Des K**, full name **Des kilometers**, is a modern competitive card game reconstructed from the traditional Chinese folk poker game "Da Lao K" (Play the Old King) in the Huainan region. It retains the decisive tactical thrill of the folk table while introducing a streamlined rule set, achieving high competitiveness and strategic depth.

The underlying lineage of "Da Lao K" is actually rooted in **Durak**, the national card game of Russia. Through refined rule modifications, *Des K* distills this cross-border, folk-rooted card combat logic into a modern competitive framework.

## 🏷️ 命名渊源与设计理念 / Design & Origins

*   **三重血脉交叠**：
    1. **俄罗斯之魂**：核心的攻防、主牌机制，源自俄罗斯国牌 **杜拉克（Durak）**。
    2. **中国之根**：Durak传入中国北方及江淮地区后，演化成本土化的“**打老K**”。
    3. **现代之形**：`Des K` 的发音与“打老K”完美谐音；同时，`Des` 源自斯诺维语，意为“**抵达**”，`Kilometers` 意为“**征途**”。结合中文副标题“**抵至极点**”，寓意将民间的古老博弈，带向竞技与策略的极致远方。

*   **Triple Heritage**:
    1. **Russian Soul**: The core attack/defense and Trump mechanics originate from the Russian national card game **Durak**.
    2. **Chinese Roots**: After reaching Northern and Central China, Durak evolved into the localized folk game "**Da Lao K**".
    3. **Modern Form**: The phonetic resemblance of `Des K` to "Da Lao K" is intentional. Additionally, `Des` in Snowlish means "**to reach**", while `Kilometers` means "**journey**". Combined with the subtitle "**To the Extreme**", it signifies taking this ancient folk duel to the ultimate heights of modern tactical competition.

## 🎲 核心玩法概览 / Core Gameplay

*   **牌具**：1副 52 张标准扑克牌（去除大小王）。
*   **开局**：双方各摸 **8张** 手牌。从牌堆底部翻出最后一张牌，其花色为本局 **“主牌”**。
*   **对抗机制**：进攻方出牌，防守方必须打出同花色点数更大的牌，或者打出**任意主牌**来接牌。（这正是 Durak 与打老K 的灵魂所在）。
*   **惩罚机制（灵魂）**：若防守方**接不住**（或选择不接），必须**收回场上所有已打出的牌**加入自己的手牌。此时**不执行攻守转换**，原进攻方补牌后继续进攻。
*   **主动停牌**：进攻方可在手握同点数牌时，随时选择“停牌”结束本回合，双方补牌并交换攻守。
*   **胜负**：任意时刻，玩家手牌变为 **0张**，该玩家**立刻获胜**。

*   **Equipment**: 1 standard 52-card deck (excluding Jokers).
*   **Setup**: Each player draws **8 cards**. Flip the bottom card of the deck; its suit becomes this round’s **"Trump"**.
*   **Combat**: The Offender plays a card. The Defender must beat it with a card of the same suit and higher rank, or by playing **any Trump card**. (This is the core soul of Durak and Da Lao K).
*   **Punishment (The Soul)**: If the Defender **fails** to beat (or chooses not to), they must **pick up all cards on the table** into their hand. **Turn switch does NOT occur**, and the original Offender continues attacking after drawing cards.
*   **Active Stop**: The Offender may choose to "Stop" at any time, ending the round, both draw cards, and switch the Offense/Defense roles.
*   **Victory**: The moment a player’s hand count reaches **0**, that player **instantly wins** the round.

## 📦 游戏版本与扩展 / Game Versions & Expansions

| 版本 / Version | 规则特点 / Features |
| :--- | :--- |
| **1v1 纯竞技版 / Pure Competitive** | 核心版本。无计分、无技能、无炸弹。纯粹的资源管理与手牌博弈。回归 Durak / 打老K 最原始的对杀快感。 / The core version. No scoring, no skills, no bombs. Pure resource management and hand-count manipulation. A return to the raw duel thrill of Durak / Da Lao K. |
| **1v1 标准版 / Standard Edition** | 在纯竞技版基础上，增加完整的**计分系统、技能卡系统（如拔卡、双倍、做主）及炸弹机制**，提供更强的策略深度与爽感。 / Adds a complete scoring system, skill cards (e.g., Block, Double, Trumpize), and the Bomb mechanic for deeper strategy and excitement. |
| **2v2 团队版 / Team Edition** | 4人分队，引入**“支援位”**与**“错位轮换”机制**，并扩展为 **92张定制牌库**。考验团队配合与战术分配。 / 4 players divided into 2 teams. Introduces **"Support"** position and **"Swap Rotation"**, utilizing a customized **92-card deck**. Tests team coordination and tactical distribution. |

## 🛠️ 游戏准备与局制 / Setup & Match System

*   **准备 / Setup**：2 名玩家（1v1）。洗牌、定主牌、各摸 8 张牌。
*   **局制 / Format**：采用 **3局2胜制**（或5局3胜制，视需要而定）。每局结束由率先清空手牌的玩家获胜，最后按总胜负决出最终赢家。
